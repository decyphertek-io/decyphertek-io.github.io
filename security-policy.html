Security Policy

Responsible Disclosure

At Decyphertek, we take security seriously. We appreciate the efforts of security researchers and the broader community in helping us maintain high security standards.

If you discover a security vulnerability in any of our systems or products, we encourage you to disclose it to us responsibly by emailing adminotaur@decyphertek.io.

Guidelines for Responsible Disclosure

  • Provide detailed information about the vulnerability, including steps to reproduce
  • Allow reasonable time for us to address the issue before public disclosure
  • Do not access, modify, or delete data beyond what is necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability
  • Do not degrade the performance of our systems or services
  • Do not violate the privacy of our users, employees, or other parties

Our Commitment

We commit to:

  • Acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 3 business days
  • Provide an estimated timeframe for addressing the vulnerability
  • Notify you when the vulnerability has been fixed
  • Give proper credit to security researchers who report vulnerabilities responsibly

Scope

This security policy applies to all Decyphertek systems, applications, and services that are publicly accessible.

Thank you for helping us keep Decyphertek secure!

Privacy Notice: We respect your privacy and do not collect or sell your data.
